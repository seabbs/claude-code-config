Create structured meeting minutes from a transcript.

## Output Format
```markdown
# Meeting Minutes - [Title] - [Date]

## Next Meeting
- **Date/Time**: [If scheduled]

## Executive Summary
[2-3 sentence overview of key decisions and outcomes]

## Action Points
### [Person Name]
- [ ] Action item with deadline

## Meeting Notes
### [Key Topic]
• Key decision or discussion point
• Important detail or context

## Attendees
- [List of participants]
```

## Guidelines
- Extract actionable items and commitments
- Group related discussions into logical sections
- Remove filler (pauses, repetitions, tangents)
- Preserve important context and reasoning
- Use bullets (•) for notes, checkboxes (- [ ]) for actions
